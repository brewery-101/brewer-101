Brew Crew: A brewery focused social media webpage

This web page will be a central hub for your night on the town. it brings back the fun of seeing a friend at the brewery while helping you navigate an increasingly disconnected world.

BrewCrew helps connect friends together for kicking back after a long day by letting your network know where you're at and what you're drinking to!
